# B2B HealthTech Portfolio Taxonomy

Single source of truth for the B2B HealthTech Portfolio framework — 8 dimensions (0-7), 46 categories.

## Purpose

The framework provides a MECE structure for analyzing B2B HealthTech platform companies. Dimension 0 covers provider business profile metrics; Dimensions 1-7 cover clinical systems, patient engagement, health data, operations, regulatory compliance, life sciences platforms, and advisory services.

---

## Dimension Definitions

### 0. Provider Profile Metrics

**Domain:** Organizational business KPIs and market presence

**Core Question:** *"What are the provider's key business indicators, scale, and market positioning?"*

**Focus:** Annual recurring revenue, employee count, customer count, geographic presence, market rankings, certifications, partnership tiers

**MECE Role:** Covers foundational provider context (who they are) — orthogonal to platform dimensions 1-7 (what they offer)

---

### 1. Clinical Systems

**Domain:** Core clinical IT platforms and patient care systems

**Core Question:** *"What clinical IT systems does the provider offer for healthcare delivery organizations?"*

**Focus:** EHR/EMR platforms, clinical decision support, medication management, laboratory and radiology information systems, perioperative and surgical IT

**MECE Role:** Covers the foundational clinical technology stack — the systems clinicians use at point of care

---

### 2. Patient Engagement

**Domain:** Patient-facing digital health tools and access platforms

**Core Question:** *"What patient-facing digital tools and engagement platforms does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Patient portals, telehealth platforms, remote patient monitoring, scheduling and access, communication tools, consumer health apps

**MECE Role:** Covers all patient-facing technology — how patients interact with providers digitally

---

### 3. Health Data & Interoperability

**Domain:** Health data exchange, analytics, and integration capabilities

**Core Question:** *"What health data management, exchange, analytics, and interoperability capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Health information exchange, FHIR/HL7 integration, clinical data warehousing, population health analytics, AI-driven clinical analytics, master patient index

**MECE Role:** Covers all data-layer capabilities — how health data flows, is stored, and generates insights

---

### 4. Revenue Cycle & Operations

**Domain:** Healthcare financial operations and supply chain management

**Core Question:** *"What revenue cycle management, claims processing, and operational systems does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Revenue cycle management, claims processing and adjudication, coding and documentation, supply chain management, workforce management

**MECE Role:** Covers the business operations layer — financial and operational systems that keep healthcare organizations running

---

### 5. Regulatory & Compliance

**Domain:** Healthcare regulatory compliance and quality reporting tools

**Core Question:** *"What regulatory compliance, quality reporting, and clinical trial management capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** HIPAA compliance tooling, clinical quality measures reporting, MDR/FDA compliance, clinical trial management, pharmacovigilance

**MECE Role:** Covers all regulatory and compliance capabilities — orthogonal to clinical functionality and operational systems

---

### 6. Life Sciences Platform

**Domain:** Life sciences technology platforms and precision medicine tools

**Core Question:** *"What life sciences technology platforms, research informatics, and precision medicine capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Drug discovery platforms, real-world evidence, genomics and precision medicine, medical device connectivity, clinical research informatics

**MECE Role:** Covers life sciences and research technology — the translational layer between clinical practice and pharmaceutical/biotech R&D

---

### 7. Advisory & Implementation

**Domain:** Health IT consulting, implementation services, and managed operations

**Core Question:** *"What consulting, implementation, training, and managed services does the provider offer for health IT?"*

**Focus:** Clinical transformation consulting, EHR implementation, interoperability consulting, training and adoption, managed health IT operations, healthcare cybersecurity, change management

**MECE Role:** Covers all professional and managed services — how providers help customers adopt and operate health IT

## MECE Validation

**Mutually Exclusive:**

- Dimension 0 (provider profile) vs. Dimensions 1-7 (platform capabilities) are categorically distinct
- Clinical Systems (point-of-care tools) vs. Patient Engagement (patient-facing tools) — provider side vs. patient side
- Health Data & Interoperability (data layer) vs. Clinical Systems (application layer) — infrastructure vs. user-facing
- Revenue Cycle & Operations (financial/operational) is orthogonal to clinical capabilities
- Regulatory & Compliance (governance/reporting) is orthogonal to both clinical and operational systems
- Life Sciences Platform (R&D/translational) vs. Clinical Systems (care delivery) — research vs. practice
- Advisory & Implementation (services) vs. Dimensions 1-6 (products/platforms)

**Collectively Exhaustive:**

- Provider Profile Metrics — Company context and market presence
- Clinical Systems — Core clinical IT for care delivery
- Patient Engagement — All patient-facing digital tools
- Health Data & Interoperability — All data exchange, storage, and analytics
- Revenue Cycle & Operations — All financial and operational systems
- Regulatory & Compliance — All regulatory, quality, and compliance tools
- Life Sciences Platform — All research, precision medicine, and translational technology
- Advisory & Implementation — All consulting, implementation, and managed services

Together these 8 dimensions (0-7) cover both provider context and the complete B2B HealthTech platform portfolio scope.
