## 3. Digital Customer Experience & New Revenue Models

> How do we transform customer experience from 616/1000 to best-in-class and enable new digital revenue streams through Energy-Sharing and Prosumer platforms?

**Executive Sponsor:** CCO / VP Customer Experience

### Investment Thesis

German energy utilities face a customer experience reckoning. The JD Power 2026 U.S. Utility Digital Experience Study reveals an overall satisfaction score of just 616 on a 1,000-point scale — a stark gap compared to wealth management apps (734), insurance (699), and retirement plans (690) [JD Power](https://www.jdpower.com/business/press-releases/2026-us-utility-digital-experience-study). While these are U.S. benchmarks, European utilities face similar challenges. The competitive risk is real: a 100-point gap separates top-performing utilities (660) from bottom performers (560), and 28% of profiled utilities still lack mobile apps despite mobile platforms consistently driving the highest satisfaction scores at 654. In a market moving toward liberalization, energy sharing, and prosumer empowerment, customer experience is no longer a back-office concern — it is the competitive battleground.

The regulatory landscape is accelerating this transformation. The Strommarktreform 2026 introduces dynamic tariffs and shorter regulatory periods, with German transmission grid fees dropping 57% through federal subsidies [Trio Advisory](https://www.trioadvisory.com/resources/grid-fee-subsidy-and-power-tax-cut-germany-2026-what-energy-intensive-sites-will-really-save). The Energy-Sharing regulation, effective July 2026, enables peer-to-peer renewable energy trading between prosumers [European Commission](https://energy.ec.europa.eu/topics/markets-and-consumers/energy-consumers-and-prosumers/protecting-and-empowering-energy-consumers_en), fundamentally disrupting the traditional utility-customer relationship. Multiple suppliers per premises become possible, creating unprecedented customer choice and switching opportunities.

Meanwhile, 83% of customers now expect real-time outage notifications [Methodia](https://www.methodia.com/blog/customer-experience-trends-in-utilities), and 89% say service quality directly influences loyalty decisions — service has surpassed price as the primary loyalty driver for many customer segments. AI-powered personalized energy recommendations drive 1-2% consumption reductions with 93% targeting accuracy [Brillion](https://brillion.ai/), while predictive churn models using gradient boosting achieve 93.2% AUC-ROC accuracy [Frontiers](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/artificial-intelligence/articles/10.3389/frai.2026.1748799/full), enabling proactive retention at scale. The economic case for CX investment is compelling: proactive churn prevention and improved digital engagement reduce acquisition costs and extend customer lifetime value in an increasingly competitive market. Fintech partnerships, subscription-based energy services, and P2P trading platforms represent entirely new revenue categories that only digitally mature utilities can capture.

### Value Chains

#### Marktreform → Kundentransformation

**Trend:** Strommarkt-Reform 2026
Germany implements comprehensive electricity market reform through Bundesnetzagentur's AgNeS initiative. Transmission fees drop 57% through subsidies. Dynamic tariffs under consideration.

**Implication:**
- **Digitale Kundenerfahrung 616/1000** — Utility CX at 616/1000, lagging cross-industry by 118+ points. Mobile apps lead at 654 but 28% of utilities still lack them.
- **Kundendaten-Strategie 360°** — Comprehensive customer data strategy enables personalized tariffs, savings recommendations, and upsell through unified 360-degree profiles.

**Possibility:**
- **Cross-Sektor Energie-Trading** — Smart contract-based B2B platforms for electricity trading between generators, consumers, and aggregators.

**Foundation Requirements:** Einheitliche Datenplattformen (data integration)

---

#### Energy-Sharing → Prosumer-Plattformen

**Trend:** Energy-Sharing-Regulierung
New EU energy sharing rules effective 17 July 2026 enable P2P renewable energy trading and multiple suppliers per premises.

**Implication:**
- **Personalisierte Energiesparp-Tipps** — AI recommendations drive 1-2% consumption reduction. 93% residential targeting accuracy.
- **Echtzeit-Ausfallmitteilungen** — 83% of customers demand real-time outage updates via SMS/Push.

**Possibility:**
- **P2P-Energiehandel Blockchain** — P2P trading normalization expected 2028-2032 via blockchain and smart contracts.
- **Utility-Fintech Partnerschaften** — Digital payments, subscriptions, and microtransactions through fintech integration.

**Foundation Requirements:** API-basierte Legacy-Modernisierung (API integration layer)

#### Churn-Reduktion → Kundenbindung

**Trend:** Strommarkt-Reform 2026
Liberalized electricity market intensifies customer switching pressure.

**Implication:**
- **Prädiktive Churn-Modelle** — Gradient boosting achieves 93.2% AUC-ROC. Profit-driven optimization emerging as best practice.
- **Omnichannel-Kundensupport** — Seamless cross-channel support with context continuity becoming standard.
- **Mobile-First Zahlungsplattformen** — Digital payment integration reduces friction and default rates.
- **Service-Loyalitäts-Treiber** — 89% of customers say service influences loyalty. Service surpassing price as primary differentiator.

**Possibility:**
- **Utility-Fintech Partnerschaften** — Embedded finance capabilities for energy-as-a-service and flexible payment.

**Foundation Requirements:** Cloud Migration & Data Lakehouse (scalable data infrastructure)

### Strategic Actions

1. **Launch or modernize utility mobile app** — With mobile apps scoring 63 points higher than mobile websites and 28% of utilities lacking apps entirely, this is the highest-impact CX investment.
2. **Deploy predictive churn models and automated retention** — 93.2% prediction accuracy enables proactive intervention before customers switch; the cost of prevention is a fraction of re-acquisition.
3. **Build P2P energy sharing platform capabilities** — July 2026 regulation creates the market; utilities without platform capabilities will be disintermediated by digital-native entrants.
4. **Implement real-time outage communication infrastructure** — 83% customer expectation creates a table-stakes requirement; laggards face measurable satisfaction penalties.
5. **Establish 360-degree customer data strategy** — Unified customer profiles are prerequisite for personalization, churn prevention, and dynamic pricing in the reformed market.
