## Company Overview

T-Systems International GmbH is a Deutsche Telekom subsidiary headquartered in Frankfurt am Main, Germany. With over 26,000 employees across 26 countries and revenue exceeding EUR 4.0 billion, T-Systems is one of Europe's leading B2B ICT service providers. Focus industries include automotive, manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, public sector, and transportation.
