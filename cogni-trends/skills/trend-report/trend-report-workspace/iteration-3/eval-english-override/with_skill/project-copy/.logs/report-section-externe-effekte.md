## T — Trends: External Effects

### ACT Horizon (0-2 Years)

#### 1. Rechenzentrum-Stromnachfrage
**Trend Overview** — Data center expansion and AI workloads drive electricity demand growth of 12-18% annually. U.S. demand projected to reach 75.8 GW by 2026. Utilities increased peak demand forecasts from 38 GW to 128 GW in a single year.

#### 2. EU-KI-Gesetz Compliance
**Trend Overview** — EU AI Act takes full effect August 2026. AI in energy grids classified as high-risk. Over 50% of organizations lack AI system inventories.

#### 3. Smart-Grid-Cybersecurity
**Trend Overview** — Grid cybersecurity market reaches $10.43B in 2026 at 14.4% CAGR. Zero-trust gaining traction in energy sector.

#### 4. Energy-Sharing-Regulierung
**Trend Overview** — New EU energy sharing rules apply from 17 July 2026. Enables peer-to-peer renewable energy trading.

#### 5. Erneuerbaren-Abregelungsmanagement
**Trend Overview** — Europe Automated Demand Response Market at $18.8B growing to $48.8B by 2032. Demand-side flexibility reduces curtailment by 61%.

### PLAN Horizon (2-5 Years)

#### 1. CCfD-Expansion 2026-2027
**Trend Overview** — Germany announces €6B for 2026 CCfD round within a broader €50B programme. Expanded to include CCS, hydrogen, and heat pumps.

#### 2. Strommarkt-Reform 2026
**Trend Overview** — Germany implements comprehensive tariff reform. Transmission fees drop 57% through federal subsidies.

#### 3. GDPR-Erweiterung Energiedaten
**Trend Overview** — Smart meter data requires GDPR compliance with granular consent management. Fines up to 4% turnover.

#### 4. Energieeffizienz-Direktive ISO 50001
**Trend Overview** — Companies above 85 TJ must implement ISO 50001 by October 2027. Energy savings potential of 10% globally.

#### 5. Carbon-Pricing-Expansion
**Trend Overview** — ETS2 expands carbon pricing to buildings and transport from 2028. Doubles EU emissions covered to 75%.
