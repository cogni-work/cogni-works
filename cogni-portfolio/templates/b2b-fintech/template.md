# B2B Fintech Portfolio Taxonomy

Single source of truth for the B2B Fintech Portfolio framework — 8 dimensions (0-7), 48 categories.

## Purpose

The framework provides a MECE structure for analyzing B2B fintech platform companies. Dimension 0 covers provider business profile metrics; Dimensions 1-7 cover platform capabilities across payments, banking, risk, capital markets, insurance, data intelligence, and advisory services.

---

## Dimension Definitions

### 0. Provider Profile Metrics

**Domain:** Organizational business KPIs and market presence

**Core Question:** *"What are the provider's key business indicators, scale, and market positioning?"*

**Focus:** Annual recurring revenue, employee count, customer count, geographic presence, market rankings, certifications, partnership tiers

**MECE Role:** Covers foundational provider context (who they are) — orthogonal to platform dimensions 1-7 (what they offer)

---

### 1. Payment Services

**Domain:** Payment processing, orchestration, and billing capabilities

**Core Question:** *"What payment processing, orchestration, and billing capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Card processing, digital wallets, cross-border payments, real-time payments, merchant acquiring, payment orchestration, billing and invoicing

**MECE Role:** Covers all payment acceptance, routing, and billing capabilities — the core fintech value chain

---

### 2. Banking & Lending Platform

**Domain:** Core banking, lending, and account management platforms

**Core Question:** *"What banking, lending, and account management platform capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Core banking systems, lending origination, deposit management, account management, open banking APIs, banking-as-a-service

**MECE Role:** Covers all banking infrastructure and lending platforms — distinct from payment processing (Dim 1)

---

### 3. Risk & Compliance

**Domain:** Financial crime prevention, credit risk, and regulatory compliance

**Core Question:** *"What risk management, compliance, and financial crime prevention capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** KYC/AML, fraud detection, credit scoring, regulatory reporting, transaction monitoring, sanctions screening

**MECE Role:** Covers all risk, compliance, and financial crime prevention — orthogonal to platform capabilities in Dim 1-2

---

### 4. Capital Markets & Trading

**Domain:** Trading platforms, order management, and market infrastructure

**Core Question:** *"What capital markets, trading, and investment management capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Trading platforms, order management, market data, portfolio management, clearing and settlement, algorithmic trading

**MECE Role:** Covers all capital markets infrastructure — distinct from retail banking (Dim 2) and payments (Dim 1)

---

### 5. Insurance Technology

**Domain:** Insurance platform capabilities across policy lifecycle

**Core Question:** *"What insurance technology capabilities does the provider offer across the policy lifecycle?"*

**Focus:** Policy administration, claims management, underwriting automation, distribution platforms, actuarial analytics

**MECE Role:** Covers all insurance-specific technology — distinct from banking (Dim 2) and capital markets (Dim 4)

---

### 6. Data & Intelligence

**Domain:** Financial analytics, embedded finance, and AI/ML capabilities

**Core Question:** *"What data analytics, embedded finance, and intelligence capabilities does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Financial analytics, embedded finance APIs, AI/ML for financial services, data aggregation, reporting and dashboards, customer insights

**MECE Role:** Covers all data-layer and intelligence capabilities — cross-cutting enablers for Dim 1-5

---

### 7. Advisory & Implementation

**Domain:** Consulting, implementation, and managed services for financial technology

**Core Question:** *"What advisory, implementation, and managed services does the provider offer?"*

**Focus:** Regulatory consulting, technology implementation, digital transformation, program management, managed operations, training and certification

**MECE Role:** Covers all post-sale services and consulting — distinct from product capabilities in Dim 1-6

## MECE Validation

**Mutually Exclusive:**

- Dimension 0 (provider profile) vs. Dimensions 1-7 (platform capabilities) are categorically distinct
- Payment Services (money movement) vs. Banking & Lending (account/loan infrastructure) vs. Capital Markets (trading/investment) are distinct financial verticals
- Risk & Compliance (prevention/detection) is orthogonal to the platforms it protects (Dim 1-2, 4-5)
- Insurance Technology (policy lifecycle) vs. Banking (deposit/loan lifecycle) are distinct regulated verticals
- Data & Intelligence (cross-cutting analytics) vs. Dimensions 1-5 (domain-specific capabilities)
- Advisory & Implementation (services) vs. Dimensions 1-6 (product capabilities)

**Collectively Exhaustive:**

- Provider Profile Metrics — Company context and market presence
- Payment Services — All payment acceptance, routing, and billing
- Banking & Lending Platform — All banking infrastructure and lending
- Risk & Compliance — All financial crime prevention and regulatory compliance
- Capital Markets & Trading — All trading, investment, and post-trade infrastructure
- Insurance Technology — All insurance platform capabilities
- Data & Intelligence — All analytics, embedded finance, and AI capabilities
- Advisory & Implementation — All consulting, implementation, and managed services

Together these 8 dimensions (0-7) cover both provider context and the complete B2B fintech platform portfolio scope.

## Cross-Cutting Attributes

These attributes apply across all dimensions:

**Target Segments:** SMB, Mid-Market, Enterprise, Tier-1 Financial Institutions

**Deployment Options:** Multi-tenant SaaS, Single-tenant, Hybrid, On-premises (regulated environments)

**Platform Ecosystem:** Card networks (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Banking partners, Cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP), Core banking vendors (Temenos, Finastra, FIS)
