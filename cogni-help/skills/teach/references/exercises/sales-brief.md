# Sales Brief: Cloud Migration for Manufacturing

## Pitch Mode

Segment (reusable template for the market)

## Target Segment

- **Market**: Mid-market discrete manufacturers (DACH)
- **Revenue range**: EUR 50M - 500M
- **Vertical**: Automotive suppliers, electronics manufacturers

## Buyer Roles

- **Economic Buyer**: CFO / VP Finance — cares about TCO, ROI, risk reduction
- **Technical Evaluator**: CTO / Head of IT — cares about architecture, integration, security
- **End Users**: Plant managers, production engineers — care about uptime, ease of use
- **Champion**: Head of Digital Transformation — initiated the evaluation

## Key Pain Points

1. Legacy on-premise ERP systems are expensive to maintain and can't scale
2. Skilled IT staff are hard to recruit in manufacturing regions
3. Supply chain disruptions require real-time visibility they don't have
4. Competitors are moving faster on digitalization

## Portfolio Data

- **Product**: CloudShift Platform
- **Key Feature**: Hybrid cloud orchestration with edge computing support
- **Proposition**: Reduce IT infrastructure costs by 35% while gaining real-time supply chain visibility

German (client-facing), English (internal methodology)
