# Marketing Brief: Cybersecurity for Mid-Market

## Brand Voice

- **Industry**: B2B technology / cybersecurity
- **Tone**: Authoritative but accessible — no fear-mongering
- **Language**: English (primary), German (secondary for DACH)

## Market

- **Segment**: Mid-market enterprises (500-5,000 employees)
- **Region**: DACH
- **Vertical focus**: Financial services, manufacturing

## GTM Path

- **TIPS Theme**: NIS2 regulatory compliance driving security investment
- **Funnel Focus**: Full-funnel (awareness through decision)
- **Portfolio Link**: Managed Detection & Response (MDR) service

## Content Strategy Seed

Start with thought leadership on NIS2 readiness, then drive demand with practical compliance checklists, convert with ROI-focused whitepapers, and enable sales with competitor battle cards.

## Priority Content

1. Blog post: "NIS2 Readiness: What Mid-Market CFOs Need to Know" (awareness)
2. LinkedIn carousel: "5 NIS2 Deadlines You Can't Afford to Miss" (engagement)
3. Whitepaper: "The Business Case for MDR in NIS2 Compliance" (conversion)
