# Research Brief: Edge Computing in Manufacturing

## Research Question

How is edge computing transforming quality control and predictive maintenance in discrete manufacturing, and what ROI are early adopters seeing?

## Scope Constraints

- Focus on discrete manufacturing (automotive, electronics, aerospace) — not process manufacturing
- Time horizon: 2023-2026 developments
- Geographic focus: DACH + North America
- Include at least 2 vendor-neutral analyst sources
- Exclude vendor whitepapers as primary sources

## Key Areas to Cover

1. Current adoption rates and maturity levels
2. Top use cases (quality inspection, predictive maintenance, digital twin)
3. ROI evidence from early adopters (quantified where possible)
4. Integration challenges with existing MES/ERP systems
5. Competitive landscape: cloud-native vs edge-first vendors

## Expected Output

A research report suitable for a client workshop on manufacturing digitalization strategy.
