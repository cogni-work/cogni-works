# B2B SaaS Portfolio Taxonomy

Single source of truth for the B2B SaaS Portfolio framework — 8 dimensions (0-7), 47 categories.

## Purpose

The framework provides a MECE structure for analyzing B2B SaaS platform companies. Dimension 0 covers provider business profile metrics; Dimensions 1-7 cover platform capabilities, ecosystem, and go-to-market.

---

## Dimension Definitions

### 0. Provider Profile Metrics

**Domain:** Organizational business KPIs and market presence

**Core Question:** *"What are the provider's key business indicators, scale, and market positioning?"*

**Focus:** Annual recurring revenue, employee count, customer count, geographic presence, market rankings, certifications, partnership tiers

**MECE Role:** Covers foundational provider context (who they are) — orthogonal to platform dimensions 1-7 (what they offer)

---

### 1. Core Platform

**Domain:** Product architecture and platform capabilities

**Core Question:** *"What are the core platform capabilities, editions, and architectural characteristics?"*

**Focus:** Product editions, multi-tenancy, platform APIs, extensibility framework, mobile capabilities, offline support, white-label/OEM options

**MECE Role:** Covers the fundamental platform product — what it is and how it's architected

---

### 2. Data & Analytics

**Domain:** Data management, reporting, and intelligence capabilities

**Core Question:** *"What data management, analytics, and intelligence capabilities does the platform provide?"*

**Focus:** Reporting dashboards, embedded analytics, data warehousing, AI/ML features, data import/export, real-time processing

**MECE Role:** Covers all data-layer capabilities built into or on top of the platform

---

### 3. Integration & Ecosystem

**Domain:** Connectivity to external systems and developer ecosystem

**Core Question:** *"What integration options, marketplace, and developer ecosystem does the platform offer?"*

**Focus:** Native integrations, app marketplace, API management, iPaaS connectors, webhooks/events, developer SDKs and tools

**MECE Role:** Covers all outward connectivity and third-party extensibility

---

### 4. Security & Compliance

**Domain:** Platform security, identity, and regulatory compliance

**Core Question:** *"What security controls, identity management, and compliance capabilities are built into the platform?"*

**Focus:** Authentication/SSO, role-based access, data encryption, compliance certifications, audit logging, data residency

**MECE Role:** Covers all trust, security, and governance capabilities

---

### 5. Customer Success & Support

**Domain:** Adoption, enablement, and ongoing support services

**Core Question:** *"What onboarding, support, and customer success services are available?"*

**Focus:** Implementation/onboarding, training programs, support tiers, customer success management, professional services, community resources

**MECE Role:** Covers all post-sale services that drive adoption and retention

---

### 6. Pricing & Packaging

**Domain:** Commercial models and packaging strategy

**Core Question:** *"What pricing models, packaging tiers, and commercial options are available?"*

**Focus:** Subscription tiers, usage-based pricing, enterprise licensing, add-on modules, free tiers, partner/reseller programs

**MECE Role:** Covers the full commercial and packaging strategy

---

### 7. Industry Solutions

**Domain:** Vertical-specific platform configurations and modules

**Core Question:** *"What industry-specific solutions, configurations, or modules are offered?"*

**Focus:** Healthcare modules, financial services compliance, retail/commerce features, manufacturing extensions

**MECE Role:** Covers all vertical specialization beyond the horizontal platform

## MECE Validation

**Mutually Exclusive:**

- Dimension 0 (provider profile) vs. Dimensions 1-7 (platform capabilities) are categorically distinct
- Core Platform (what it is) vs. Data & Analytics (what insights it provides) vs. Integration (how it connects)
- Security & Compliance (trust/governance) is orthogonal to functional capabilities
- Customer Success (post-sale services) vs. Pricing (commercial models) are distinct go-to-market dimensions
- Industry Solutions (vertical specialization) vs. Dimensions 1-6 (horizontal capabilities)

**Collectively Exhaustive:**

- Provider Profile Metrics — Company context and market presence
- Core Platform — Product architecture and capabilities
- Data & Analytics — All data-layer functionality
- Integration & Ecosystem — All external connectivity
- Security & Compliance — All trust and governance
- Customer Success & Support — All post-sale services
- Pricing & Packaging — All commercial models
- Industry Solutions — All vertical specialization

Together these 8 dimensions (0-7) cover both provider context and the complete B2B SaaS platform portfolio scope.
