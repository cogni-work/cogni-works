# B2B MarTech Portfolio Taxonomy

Single source of truth for the B2B MarTech Portfolio framework — 8 dimensions (0-7), 45 categories.

## Purpose

The framework provides a MECE structure for analyzing B2B marketing technology platform companies. Dimension 0 covers provider business profile metrics; Dimensions 1-7 cover platform capabilities across customer data, campaign automation, content, advertising, analytics, privacy, and services.

---

## Dimension Definitions

### 0. Provider Profile Metrics

**Domain:** Organizational business KPIs and market presence

**Core Question:** *"What are the provider's key business indicators, scale, and market positioning?"*

**Focus:** Annual recurring revenue, employee count, customer count, geographic presence, market rankings, certifications, partnership tiers

**MECE Role:** Covers foundational provider context (who they are) — orthogonal to platform dimensions 1-7 (what they offer)

---

### 1. Customer Data & Identity

**Domain:** Customer data infrastructure, identity resolution, and audience management

**Core Question:** *"What customer data management, identity resolution, and audience capabilities does the platform provide?"*

**Focus:** CDP, identity graphs, audience segmentation, data enrichment, consent management, data clean rooms

**MECE Role:** Covers the foundational data layer — who the customers are and how they are identified, segmented, and governed

---

### 2. Campaign & Marketing Automation

**Domain:** Campaign execution, orchestration, and marketing workflow automation

**Core Question:** *"What campaign management, marketing automation, and journey orchestration capabilities does the platform provide?"*

**Focus:** Email marketing, automation workflows, journey builders, A/B testing, lead scoring, social media management

**MECE Role:** Covers the execution layer — how marketing actions are planned, triggered, and optimized

---

### 3. Content & Experience

**Domain:** Content management, digital experiences, and personalization

**Core Question:** *"What content management, digital experience, and personalization capabilities does the platform provide?"*

**Focus:** CMS, digital asset management, personalization engines, commerce experiences, search, localization

**MECE Role:** Covers the experience layer — what content is delivered and how it is personalized across channels

---

### 4. Advertising & Media

**Domain:** Paid media buying, programmatic advertising, and measurement

**Core Question:** *"What advertising technology, media buying, and attribution capabilities does the platform provide?"*

**Focus:** Programmatic DSP/SSP, search ads, social ads, retail media, attribution, media planning

**MECE Role:** Covers the paid media layer — how advertising inventory is bought, sold, and measured

---

### 5. Analytics & Intelligence

**Domain:** Marketing analytics, customer journey insights, and AI/ML capabilities

**Core Question:** *"What marketing analytics, customer intelligence, and AI/ML capabilities does the platform provide?"*

**Focus:** Marketing analytics, journey analytics, AI/ML for marketing, predictive modeling, dashboards

**MECE Role:** Covers the intelligence layer — what insights and predictions are derived from marketing data

---

### 6. Privacy & Compliance

**Domain:** Data privacy, consent management, and regulatory compliance for marketing

**Core Question:** *"What privacy, consent, and compliance capabilities does the platform provide for marketing data?"*

**Focus:** Cookie/consent management, GDPR/CCPA compliance, brand safety, data governance, identity deprecation

**MECE Role:** Covers the governance layer — how marketing data is collected, stored, and used in compliance with regulations

---

### 7. Services & Enablement

**Domain:** Professional services, consulting, and platform enablement

**Core Question:** *"What implementation, consulting, creative, and training services are available?"*

**Focus:** Implementation services, strategic consulting, creative services, training programs, managed campaigns

**MECE Role:** Covers all services that drive adoption, implementation, and ongoing platform value realization

## MECE Validation

**Mutually Exclusive:**

- Dimension 0 (provider profile) vs. Dimensions 1-7 (platform capabilities) are categorically distinct
- Customer Data & Identity (who the audience is) vs. Campaign & Marketing Automation (how they are engaged) vs. Content & Experience (what they see)
- Advertising & Media (paid channels) vs. Campaign & Marketing Automation (owned channels) are distinct channel types
- Analytics & Intelligence (measuring outcomes) is orthogonal to execution dimensions 1-4
- Privacy & Compliance (governance/regulation) is orthogonal to functional capabilities
- Services & Enablement (human-delivered) vs. Dimensions 1-6 (technology-delivered)

**Collectively Exhaustive:**

- Provider Profile Metrics — Company context and market presence
- Customer Data & Identity — All customer data infrastructure and identity capabilities
- Campaign & Marketing Automation — All campaign execution and automation capabilities
- Content & Experience — All content management and personalization capabilities
- Advertising & Media — All paid media buying and measurement capabilities
- Analytics & Intelligence — All analytics, AI/ML, and reporting capabilities
- Privacy & Compliance — All privacy, compliance, and governance capabilities
- Services & Enablement — All professional services and training

Together these 8 dimensions (0-7) cover both provider context and the complete B2B MarTech platform portfolio scope.
