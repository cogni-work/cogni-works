## 5. Dekarbonisierung & Nachhaltige Infrastruktur-Investition

> How do we leverage carbon contracts, green financing, and the €500B infrastructure fund for decarbonization and sustainable value creation?

**Executive Sponsor:** CSO / VP Strategie & Nachhaltigkeit

### Investment Thesis

Germany's €6 billion CCfD auction for 2026 — now expanded to include CCS, CCU, hydrogen, and industrial heat pumps — represents a transformational funding mechanism for decarbonization [Fuel Cells Works](https://fuelcellsworks.com/2025/10/06/green-investment/germany-opens-pre-bid-phase-for-6bn-2026-climate-contracts-tender-adds-ccs-and-hydrogen-scope). With Germany potentially setting aside €50 billion for CCfD subsidies overall [Hydrogen Insight](https://www.hydrogeninsight.com/policy/hydrogen-in-industry-germany-to-set-aside-roughly-50bn-for-carbon-contracts-for-difference-subsidy-scheme/2-1-1462051), and a separate $7 billion hydrogen plan for 2026 [Industrial Info](https://www.industrialinfo.com/iirenergy/industry-news/article/germany-outlines-7-billion-hydrogen-plan-for-2026--348044), the scale of public investment in decarbonization is unprecedented.

The €500 billion infrastructure fund adds another layer: approximately €100 billion allocated to the Climate and Transformation Fund, with €58 billion deployed in 2026 alone [Clean Energy Wire](https://www.cleanenergywire.org/factsheets/qa-germanys-eu500-bln-infrastructure-fund-whats-it-climate-and-energy). This creates a once-in-a-generation opportunity for utilities to fund grid modernization, renewable integration, and green hydrogen production with public backing.

European utilities are already accelerating: €173 billion in capex planned for 2026 (+6% YoY), with network utilities' investment reaching 164% of EBITDA [ING Think](https://think.ing.com/articles/energy-european-utilities-2026-steady-currents/). The Deutschlandfonds adds €30 billion for startup innovation and infrastructure [Bundesfinanzministerium](https://www.bundesfinanzministerium.de/Content/DE/Pressemitteilungen/Finanzpolitik/2025/12/2025-12-18-deutschlandfonds-startet.html). Smart metering — the data backbone for all optimization — is approaching $20 billion in global revenues. The strategic imperative: position early for public funding access while building the digital infrastructure that makes investments productive.

### Strategic Actions

1. **Prepare CCfD auction bids for green hydrogen projects** — €6B auction with 15-year price guarantees creates long-term revenue certainty.
2. **Qualify grid modernization projects for €500B infrastructure fund** — €58B deployed in 2026 alone; early applications capture allocation.
3. **Develop green hydrogen production scenarios** — $7B Germany hydrogen plan signals national commitment.
4. **Build carbon accounting and optimization capabilities** — ETS expansion to heating and transport by 2028 requires dynamic carbon cost modeling.
5. **Leverage Deutschlandfonds for energy innovation spin-offs** — €30B fund opens Q2–Q3 2026 for first fundings.
