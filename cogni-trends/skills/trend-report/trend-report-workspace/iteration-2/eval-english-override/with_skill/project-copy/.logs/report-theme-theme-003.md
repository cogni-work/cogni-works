## 3. Digitale Kundenerfahrung & Neue Erlösmodelle

> How do we transform CX from 616/1000 to best-in-class and enable new digital revenue through energy sharing and prosumer platforms?

**Executive Sponsor:** CCO / VP Kundenerfahrung

### Investment Thesis

German utilities face a customer experience reckoning. The 2026 JD Power study reveals average utility digital satisfaction at just 616 on a 1,000-point scale, with a 100-point gap between leaders (660) and laggards (560) [JD Power](https://www.jdpower.com/business/press-releases/2026-us-utility-digital-experience-study). Mobile apps score 654 — 63 points above mobile web — yet 28% of utilities still offer no app at all. This mediocrity becomes existential when combined with regulatory disruption: the Strommarktreform 2026 introduces time-variable pricing and abolishes fixed feed-in tariffs, while preliminary grid tariffs show a 57% average reduction [Trio Advisory](https://www.trioadvisory.com/resources/grid-fee-subsidy-and-power-tax-cut-germany-2026-what-energy-intensive-sites-will-really-save).

Simultaneously, EU energy sharing rules take effect on 17 July 2026, enabling multi-supplier premises and decentralized electricity trading [Exnaton](https://www.exnaton.ai/en/blog/energy-market-europe-2026). These regulations transform the utility-customer relationship from a captive monopoly to a competitive platform play. Utilities that cannot offer personalized tariffs, real-time outage communication (demanded by 83% of customers [Methodia](https://www.methodia.com/blog/customer-experience-trends-in-utilities)), and modern payment experiences will hemorrhage customers to agile competitors.

The strategic response requires a 360-degree customer data foundation. Unified customer data enables AI-driven segmentation, predictive churn models, and personalized energy saving recommendations that reduce consumption by 8–12% while strengthening loyalty. Service quality has become the top loyalty driver, with 89% of customers saying it influences their decisions [Methodia](https://www.methodia.com/blog/customer-experience-trends-in-utilities). Fintech partnerships and P2P energy trading platforms create entirely new revenue streams beyond commodity energy sales.

**Trend:** Strommarkt-Reform 2026 — Germany replacing market premium model with CfDs; grid tariffs dropping 57%.

**Implication:**
- **Digitale Kundenerfahrung 616/1000** — 100-point gap between leaders and laggards signals massive optimization potential.
- **Kundendaten-Strategie 360°** — Single customer view enables personalized tariffs and retention.

**Possibility:**
- **Cross-Sektor Energie-Trading** — B2B platforms for electricity trading emerging through smart contracts.

### Strategic Actions

1. **Launch a utility mobile app (or upgrade existing)** — Mobile apps score 63 points higher than web; 28% of utilities lack one entirely.
2. **Build energy sharing platform capabilities before July 2026** — EU regulation creates an imminent deadline for P2P trading readiness.
3. **Implement 360-degree Customer Data Platform** — Single customer view is the prerequisite for personalization, churn prediction, and dynamic pricing.
4. **Deploy real-time outage notification system** — 83% customer demand makes this a hygiene factor, not a differentiator.
5. **Develop predictive churn models and proactive retention campaigns** — Market liberalization makes customer retention a competitive survival issue.
